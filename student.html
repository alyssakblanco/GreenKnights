Electronic Notetaking

Ditch those notebooks and use your laptop or tablet to take all your notes. Saves space in your backpack, prevents clutter, and you can sync your notes across devices!

Upcycled Supplies

Plastic pens? Shop for recycled ones, and make sure to recycle the plastic when you’re done.

Recycle Old Supplies

All your old binders full of paper and notebooks can be recycled, along with your plastic supplies.

Electronic Mail

Tired of getting tons of wasted paper junk in your mailbox? Save paper by opting out of junk mail letters and opt for e-staments from you service providers!

Smart Power

Use a smart power strip for your electronic devices. They reduce your power usage, and therefore your carbon footprint, by shutting down devices that are in standby mode.
