Bathroom Essentials

Plastic bathroom products end up in a landfill indefinitely, or in our oceans. Swap out the following items for ones made of natural materials, like wood or cotton.

Mix your own toothpaste, mouthwash, and deodorant.

Normally these products come in plastic containers that are non-recyclable. Click here to learn how!

Try using a shampoo bar instead of shampoo from a plastic bottle.

Zero waste shampoo and conditioners!

Replace plastic soap bottles with bar soap that comes in non-plastic packaging!

Swap disposable razors for a safety razor!

Safety razors are made of stainless steel. The blades can be recycled at the end of their life, and the razor itself can last years! Safety Razor guide

Use 100% recycled toilet paper!

Make your own makeup remover!
