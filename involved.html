Let's get greener, together

Interested in getting involved in the Green movement here at UCF? Here are some resources that may help:

UCF Initiatives


UCF Sustainability Initiatives

UCF Sustainability initiatives provides many resources for getting involved on campus. Here you can find events, a newsletter, videos, and a calendar for upcoming events.

Find out more

Electric Bike and Light Electric Vehicle Association

UCF's Electric Bike and Light Electric Vehicle Association aims to spread awareness of the environmental, health, and accessibility benefits of cycling, e-biking, and light electric vehicle use. They also promote safe riding practices and public advocacy for electric bicycle and light electric vehicle usage within the UCF community.

Find out more

Environmental Aesthetics Association

The Environmental Aesthetics Association believes that any possible action should be taken at the University of Central Florida towards sustainability, environmental health, and aesthetic pleasure.

Find out more

Knights for Wildlife Conservation

Knights for Wildlife Conservation (KWC) inspires student involvement in conserving the habitats of wild animals and plant life through volunteer activities.

Find out more

Knights Go Green

Knights go Green aims to inspire people to care for and protect their habitat, implement green programs around campus, clean areas at risk of detrimental contamination, and improve areas that have already been negatively impacted.

Find out more

UCF Arboretum

The UCF Arboretum's mission is to cultivate, celebrate, and communicate the value of plants, ecosystems, and biodiversity to human well-being.

Find out more
