Using Resuable Towels

Using reusbale towels to for cleaning allows people to be less wasteful when cleaning around their living situation. You can use them over and over again making them less wasteful than paper towels or even the less wasteful alternative reusable bamboo paper towels. You can get reusable clothes from any old preused piece of fabric in the house that you may have or you can even go out and buy one.

No Need to Recycle When You Can Reuse

Instead of going out and constantly buying bottles of the different cleaning supplies that the average household uses you can buy the product that you want in a bigger bulk supply and just refill reusable bottles that way you would be wasting less plastic plus if you start using alternatives to those cleaning supplies then you could fill the bottles with those.
